Welcome! I am a Ph.D. student in the Jurisprudence and Social Policy Program at U.C. Berkeley and a J.D. student Yale Law School. My research interests are in administrative law, law and globalization, the legal profession, and contemporary Chinese law and politics. My work has appeared or is forthcoming in *The China Quarterly*, *Law & Social Inquiry*, and the  *Journal of Empirical Legal Studies*.
